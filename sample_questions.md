# Sample Questions for Enterprise Intelligence Engine

## Single Domain — HR
1. Show employees by department with monthly income
2. What is the average monthly income by department?
3. How many employees have left the company (attrition = Yes)?
4. Show attrition rate by department
5. Which job role has the highest average monthly income?
6. What is the average years at company for employees who left vs stayed?
7. Show employees with overtime and their performance rating
8. How many employees work in Research & Development?
9. Average salary hike percentage by education field
10. Show correlation between work life balance and attrition

## Single Domain — Tickets
11. How many high priority tickets are unresolved?
12. Show all open tickets sorted by priority
13. What is the average resolution time by priority level?
14. Count of tickets by customer segment
15. Which issue type has the highest average CSAT score?
16. Show tickets with urgent priority that are still in progress
17. Average resolution time by channel and region

## Single Domain — Maintenance
18. How many machines have failed (target = 1)?
19. Show failure count by machine type (L, M, H)
20. What is the average tool wear for failed vs non-failed machines?
21. Which failure type is most common across all machine types?

## Single Domain — Sales
22. Total sales by category
23. Show top 10 customers by total sales
24. Average sales per order by segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
25. Which region has the highest total sales?
26. Show sales by category and region

## Cross-Domain (2 Domains)
27. Show departments with highest attrition and their average monthly income
28. Compare total sales by region against ticket volume by region
29. Which departments have the most employees working overtime, and what are the top failure types in maintenance?
30. Show employees in Sales who earn above the department average, and list their related support tickets
31. What is the average resolution time for tickets in regions where sales are highest?
32. Find machine failure rates by type and compare against employee overtime patterns in HR
33. Show revenue by region and count of urgent tickets in each region

## Cross-Domain (3 Domains)
34. How does employee attrition in each department relate to ticket volume and average resolution time?
35. Show total sales per region, number of open tickets, and machine failure counts side by side
36. Compare departments by attrition rate, average monthly income, and ticket volume
37. Which regions have both high sales revenue and high ticket volumes — what are their top failure modes?

## Full Interdependency (4+ Domains + Emails)
38. Analyze cross-domain risk: departments with highest attrition, their overtime trends, ticket volumes by priority, and search emails for mentions of "resignation" or "turnover"

39. Give me a unified enterprise overview — show record counts across HR, tickets, maintenance, sales, and emails, with the average monthly income per department, unresolved ticket counts by priority, failure rates by machine type, and total sales by category — all in one analysis

40. Analyze the interdependency across all enterprise domains: how does employee attrition in each HR department relate to ticket volume and average resolution time, do departments with higher machine failure rates also have more open tickets, and what is the sales performance (total revenue and top categories) in regions with high attrition — then search emails for any communications about cross-departmental escalations in those high-risk areas

41. Identify high-risk zones: departments with attrition > 20%, their average monthly income, the number of high-priority tickets, and top machine failure types. Also check emails for escalations or complaints from those departments

42. Compare high-attrition departments vs low-attrition departments across all metrics: ticket volume, resolution time, machine failure rate, sales performance in their region, and email sentiment about workplace issues
